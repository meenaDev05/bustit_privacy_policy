Privacy Policy for Bust It

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bust It ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application. We built Bust It to collect as little personal information as possible. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

By downloading, installing, or using Bust It, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

You can use Bust It in one of two ways:

  • Guest mode: You choose a display username. No email, password, or other personal identifier is required or collected.
  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google account's display name, email address, and profile photo URL (if available) from Google, via Firebase Authentication, solely to create and identify your player account.

We do not collect your real name, phone number, physical address, date of birth, or any other personal information beyond what is described above.

1.2 Gameplay data

To run the game, we store the following gameplay data, associated with your account or guest profile:

  • Username / display name
  • Quiz answers, scores, points, and streaks
  • Badges/medals earned
  • Avatar customization choices (selected in-app, not a photo of you)
  • Timestamps of activity (e.g., last answered date, account creation date)

This data is stored using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication).

1.3 Advertising data

Bust It displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), IP address, general device information, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads. AdMob may also use this data to personalize ads if permitted by your device and account settings.

We do not control what data AdMob collects. For details on what Google collects and how it is used, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and the AdMob Privacy & Security Center .

1.4 Automatically collected technical data

Like most apps, certain technical information may be collected automatically by our infrastructure providers (Firebase, Google Play services) for operational and security purposes, such as crash logs, device model, OS version, and app version. This is used only to maintain and improve app stability and is not used to personally identify you beyond what is described above.

We do not collect: precise location, contacts, photos/media from your device, microphone or camera data, financial information, or health data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Create and maintain your player profile and game progress
  • Display leaderboards, streaks, points, and unlocked medals
  • Authenticate you across sessions (Google Sign-In users)
  • Display and measure advertising through AdMob
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app performance and stability
  • Prevent cheating and abuse (e.g., detecting answer manipulation)

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for any purpose unrelated to operating the App.

4. Children's Privacy

Bust It is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can delete it.

If our App is listed on Google Play as suitable for a mixed or general audience, ad serving complies with Google Play's Families policies and applicable laws such as COPPA, where relevant.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (see Section 7), your profile data and associated gameplay history are deleted from our active systems, except where retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes, or backups that are purged on a routine cycle.

6. Data Security

We use Firebase's built-in security infrastructure, including authenticated access rules, to protect your data. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Guest users: Since no personal information is tied to a guest account, you may simply stop using the App or uninstall it to discontinue use. Guest game progress may not be recoverable once the app is uninstalled or local data is cleared.
  • Google Sign-In users: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable time.
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings (e.g., Android Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization") or by resetting your Advertising ID.
  • Sign out: You may sign out of your Google account in the App at any time via the settings menu.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States, as part of Google Firebase's global infrastructure. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
